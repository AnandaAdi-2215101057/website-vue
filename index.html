About Me

I'm Ananda Adi

Hello! I'm Ananda Adi, and I embarked on my journey into the world of Information Technology in 2022, armed with determination and a hunger for knowledge. While some may have started with a strong foundation in IT, I took my first steps with no prior experience, and it has been an exhilarating ride ever since. Alumni from SD Imanuel, SMP 2 Kuta Utara and SMA 1 Kuta Utara

My decision to dive into IT was driven by a passion for problem-solving, technology's ever-evolving nature, and the endless possibilities it offers. I saw it as an opportunity to challenge myself and explore a field that fascinated me but remained uncharted territory. Starting from scratch meant facing a steep learning curve, but I embraced it as an exciting challenge rather than an obstacle.

As a musician, I've always been captivated by the power of melodies, rhythms, and harmonies to convey emotions and tell stories. My music allowed me to connect with people on a deeply personal level. It was during my musical endeavors that I stumbled upon the fascinating world of technology. I became intrigued by how technology could enhance the creative process and enable me to share my music with a wider audience.

In parallel with my musical pursuits, I dabbled in novel writing. The written word offered me a different canvas for storytelling, one where I could craft intricate narratives and explore the depths of imagination. Little did I know that my transition into IT would also expand my horizons as a writer, offering me new tools and platforms to express my creativity.

What I Do

Copywriter

I'm an amateur copywriter, and my goal is to help you create words that make people interested in what you have to say. Whether it's making your products sound exciting, writing blog posts that explain things clearly, or coming up with catchy social media posts, I'm here to make your message sound great.

UI/UX Designer

I'm an amateur UI/UX designer, and my focus is making things on the internet look and feel better. UI, or User Interface, is like the clothes your website or app wears - it should be attractive and easy to navigate. UX, or User Experience, is about making sure people enjoy using your website or app. I'm learning how to create designs that are user-friendly and visually appealing, so your online spaces become more inviting and enjoyable for everyone who visits.

Story Writer

As an amateur short story writer, I'm deeply passionate about the art of storytelling and the magic of crafting compelling narratives. While I may not have a long list of published works under my belt, I pour my heart and imagination into every story I create. Writing is my way of exploring the vast landscapes of human emotions and the complexities of life, and I'm excited to share my evolving skills with readers. I believe that even as an amateur, I can evoke a sense of wonder, laughter, and introspection through my stories.
